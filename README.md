# March-Competition-Project---Ursuline-
Our program addresses the widespread issue of students and young adults struggling to understand where their money goes, how daily choices affect long‑term stability, and how to build healthier financial habits. Many people track income and expenses inconsistently, rely on guesswork, or feel overwhelmed by budgeting tools that are too complex or too generic to be useful.

Features:
- Balance calculations to show the user exactly where they stand
- Itemized spending lists to track purchaces
- loan interpretation to break down the costs and long term implacations of a potential loan
- Monthly budget creation tailored to acctual habits
- Lifestyle impact calculator that models how reducing certain expenses improves savings
- Expense trend analysis to highlight increases, decreases, and seasonal patterns.
- Personalized suggestions that guide users toward healthier, sustainable financial behavior
